Healthcare and laboratory facilities have mechanical requirements that go beyond comfort — they're a matter of compliance, safety, and operational continuity.

- **Operating rooms and clean rooms** — Positive pressure, HEPA filtration, temperature and humidity control
- **Pharmacy and medication storage** — Temperature monitoring and alarming
- **Laboratory exhaust** — Fume hood systems, dedicated exhaust, makeup air
- **General HVAC** — Patient and staff comfort, ventilation code compliance
- **Redundancy** — Backup systems, lead/lag configurations, emergency power coordination
