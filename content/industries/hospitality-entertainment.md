- **Movie theaters** — High-capacity cooling for auditoriums, concession refrigeration
- **Event venues** — Temporary and permanent HVAC for variable occupancy loads
- **Wine bars and tasting rooms** — Precision cellar cooling, display case refrigeration
- **Restaurants within hospitality** — Full kitchen mechanical scope

We schedule work around events and guest occupancy to eliminate disruption.
