We work with public agencies and school districts on both new construction and ongoing maintenance. Public sector projects require prevailing wage compliance, proper documentation, and contractors who can navigate the bid process — we've done it.

## Clients

- **Riverside Unified School District** — HVAC service and installation across campus facilities
- **Municipal buildings and public facilities** — City offices, community centers, public works buildings

## What we handle

- **New construction and TI** — Mechanical scope for new school buildings, portable classrooms, and public facility renovations
- **Preventive maintenance** — Scheduled PM programs for entire campus or facility portfolios
- **Emergency service** — Priority dispatch for classrooms, server rooms, and occupied public spaces
- **Equipment replacement** — Rooftop unit changeouts, boiler replacements, controls upgrades
- **Electrical** — C-10 licensed electrical work as part of mechanical scope, reducing sub-contractor coordination

## Compliance

We handle prevailing wage requirements, certified payroll, and all documentation needed for public works contracts. Licensed and insured for public agency work in California.
