Wine and specialty storage require tighter environmental tolerances than standard comfort cooling. A few degrees off or a humidity swing can damage inventory worth more than the system itself.

- **Wine cellars and wine bars** — Split systems and ducted cooling sized to room volume, insulation, and glass exposure
- **Commercial wine storage** — Multi-zone temperature control for red, white, and sparkling
- **Cigar rooms and humidors** — Humidity-controlled environments for retail and lounge settings
- **Art and document storage** — Stable temperature and humidity for preservation

We install, service, and monitor these systems — including remote alarming when conditions drift out of range.
