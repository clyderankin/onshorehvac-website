Indoor cultivation demands precise environmental control — temperature, humidity, airflow, and CO2 levels directly affect yield and quality. A generic HVAC install won't cut it.

We design and install climate systems purpose-built for grow environments:

- **Cooling and dehumidification** — Sized to actual canopy load, not square footage rules of thumb
- **Ventilation and air exchange** — Positive/negative pressure control, carbon filtration, odor management
- **Humidity control** — Dedicated dehumidifiers integrated with HVAC, not fighting against it
- **CO2 supplementation** — System integration with environmental controllers
- **Redundancy** — Backup cooling to protect crop value when primary systems go down

We've built systems for facilities from startup rooms to large-scale commercial operations across Southern California.
