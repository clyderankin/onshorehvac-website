Fitness environments generate massive internal heat loads from occupants and equipment. Standard office HVAC can't keep up — you need systems sized for peak class capacity, not empty-room conditions.

- **Gyms and fitness centers** — High-volume air handling, ventilation for equipment floors and locker rooms
- **Boutique studios** — Yoga, pilates, cycling, fencing, martial arts — each with different temperature and airflow needs
- **Athletic training facilities** — Spot cooling, exhaust, and fresh air systems
- **Locker rooms and showers** — Exhaust, dehumidification, and makeup air

We work with studio owners, gym chains, and landlords on both new buildouts and upgrades to existing spaces across Southern California.
