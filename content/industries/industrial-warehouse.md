Industrial facilities have unique thermal loads — process heat, large open spans, loading dock traffic, and variable occupancy.

- **Process cooling** — Chilled water systems, glycol loops, heat exchangers
- **Warehouse ventilation** — Destratification fans, exhaust systems, makeup air
- **Cold storage** — Blast freezers, controlled atmosphere rooms, dock leveler seals
- **Manufacturing HVAC** — Spot cooling, clean room supply, paint booth tempering
- **Loading dock** — Air curtains, dock seals, infiltration management
