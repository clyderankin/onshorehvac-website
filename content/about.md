## The short version

Onshore Mechanical is a family-run commercial and residential HVAC contractor based in Huntington Beach, California. Founded by **Brock Dains**, we keep businesses comfortable and equipment running across Southern California.

## How we got here

Brock started this company the way most good mechanical contractors start — by showing up, doing the work right, and earning repeat business one building at a time. That hasn't changed. We're still family-operated, still hands-on, and still the company that picks up the phone when your system goes down on a Friday night.

We've grown from a small service outfit into a full design-build mechanical contractor holding three licenses: HVAC, C-10 electrical, and sheet metal fabrication. That means we handle the whole job — system design, new construction, ductwork fabrication in our own shop, electrical wiring in-house, and ongoing service — without farming work out to subs.

## What we believe

**Show up when you say you will.** Sounds basic. It's not. In this industry, half the battle is finding a contractor who actually returns calls and keeps appointments.

**Be honest about what the building needs.** We don't sell equipment you don't need. If a repair makes more sense than a replacement, we'll tell you — even when the replacement pays us more.

**Do it right the first time.** Our reputation is built on referrals. Every job we cut corners on is a customer we lose. We don't cut corners.

## The team

We're a crew of experienced commercial HVAC technicians, electricians, and sheet metal fabricators. Most of our guys have been with us for years. When we send a tech to your facility, they've likely been there before and already know your equipment.
