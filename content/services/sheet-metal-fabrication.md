We manufacture ductwork in our own sheet metal shop. That means custom fittings built to your job's exact dimensions — not generic catalog pieces forced to fit.

## Why it matters

**Better fit** — Field-measured, shop-fabricated, delivered ready to install. Custom transitions and offsets that actually match the existing conditions in your building.

**Faster turnaround** — We don't wait on a third-party fab shop's queue. When we need a piece, we build it.

**Lower cost** — No middleman markup on fabrication. You're paying for material and labor, not a sub's profit margin on top.

## What we build

- Rectangular and spiral ductwork
- Custom plenums and return air boxes
- Transitions, offsets, and reducers
- Kitchen exhaust hoods and grease ducts
- Equipment curbs and roof penetration flashings
- Specialty fittings for retrofit and tight-space installations

Every piece is built to SMACNA standards and sized to match the system's airflow and static pressure requirements.
