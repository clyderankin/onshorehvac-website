Unplanned downtime is the most expensive maintenance there is. Our PM programs keep systems operating within spec and catch failures before they become emergencies.

## Program structure

Each preventive maintenance agreement is scoped to your equipment inventory. We maintain a complete asset register for your facility — model numbers, install dates, service history, and warranty status.

**Typical quarterly tasks:**
- Filter replacement
- Coil inspection and cleaning
- Belt tension and wear check
- Refrigerant charge verification
- Electrical connections and contactors
- Thermostat calibration
- Drain line clearing
- Condenser and evaporator performance check

## Reporting

Every visit generates a service report documenting work completed, measurements taken, and any conditions requiring attention. Reports are delivered digitally within 24 hours.

## Emergency priority

PM clients receive priority scheduling for emergency service calls — including after-hours and weekends.
