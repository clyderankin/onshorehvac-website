Most HVAC contractors call in a separate electrical sub. We hold our own C-10 electrical license, so the same crew that installs your unit also wires it.

## What this means for your project

**Faster timelines** — No waiting on an electrical sub's schedule. We wire disconnects, run conduit, and terminate controls as part of the HVAC install.

**Single point of accountability** — One contractor, one permit, one warranty. No finger-pointing between trades when something doesn't work.

**Cost savings** — Eliminating a sub means eliminating their markup and mobilization costs.

## Scope

- Electrical disconnects and circuit whips for HVAC and refrigeration equipment
- Panel upgrades when existing service can't handle new equipment loads
- Controls wiring — thermostats, BAS, economizers, VFDs
- 3-phase power for commercial rooftop units and compressor racks
- Motor replacement and compressor electrical
- Code-compliant permitting and inspections
