We design and install commercial HVAC systems for new construction and major renovations. Every system is engineered to the facility's actual thermal load — not oversized from a rule of thumb.

## Scope

**New construction** — Coordinate with GCs and architects from design through commissioning. We handle mechanical drawings, equipment selection, ductwork fabrication, and controls integration.

**Retrofit & replacement** — Upgrade aging equipment without disrupting operations. We phase installations around your schedule and maintain temporary cooling/heating as needed.

## Equipment

We are brand-agnostic. Equipment is selected based on load calculations, efficiency targets, serviceability, and parts availability — not manufacturer incentives.

## Controls

All installations include commissioning and verification of building automation system (BAS) integration. We don't hand off until setpoints are met and the system is operating within spec.
