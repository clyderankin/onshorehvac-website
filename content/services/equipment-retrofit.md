Aging equipment costs more to run, more to repair, and fails at the worst time. We plan and execute equipment upgrades on your timeline — phased to keep the facility operational.

## When to retrofit

- Equipment past manufacturer's expected service life
- Repair costs exceeding 50% of replacement value
- Refrigerant no longer available or economically viable
- Energy costs significantly above benchmark
- Code or compliance changes requiring system modifications

## Planning

We survey existing conditions, document operational requirements, and deliver a proposal with equipment specifications, projected energy savings, and a phased installation schedule that minimizes downtime.
