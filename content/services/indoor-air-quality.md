Indoor air quality affects occupant health, productivity, and regulatory standing. We assess, design, and install ventilation and filtration systems to meet ASHRAE standards and local code requirements.

## Services

**Ventilation assessment** — Measure existing airflow rates against ASHRAE 62.1 requirements. Identify under-ventilated zones and recommend corrections.

**Filtration upgrades** — Upgrade to higher-MERV or HEPA filtration where systems can support the added static pressure. Includes fan capacity verification.

**Monitoring** — Install CO2, particulate, and humidity sensors tied to building automation for continuous air quality tracking.

**Kitchen and specialty exhaust** — Design and install exhaust and makeup air systems for commercial kitchens, labs, and process areas.
